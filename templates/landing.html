Frequently Asked Questions

What is TheAIrticle?

TheAIrticle is an AI-powered article generator that allows users to input a topic, keywords, and desired length to automatically generate articles.

How can I customize the generated content?

Users can easily customize the AI-generated content by using the provided editing tools. You can edit, format, and make changes to the generated articles as per your requirements.

What formats can I export my articles in?

TheAIrticle supports multiple export formats, including DOCX, PDF, Markdown (MD), and plain text (TXT). You can choose the format that suits your needs when saving your finalized articles.

Is there a user authentication system?

Yes, TheAIrticle provides a user authentication system. Users can sign up and log in using a simple email and password-based authentication process, which ensures data security and personalized access to their content.

Where can I access my saved articles?

You can access your saved articles through the user dashboard. The dashboard allows you to view and manage your saved content and initiate new content generation requests easily.
